To manage your Flõs, click the "My Flõs" link in the nav bar. If you've already built some Flõs or added Flõs from the Gallery, you should see them displayed here.

Sorting Your Flõs

You can sort your Flõs using the options in the upper right corner of the My Flõs view. You can look at your most recent Flõs, sort by name, or sort by creator. You can also choose if you want to see All Flõs in your organization, just Flõs that you created, only active Flõs or only inactive Flõs.

Editing Flõs

On each Flõ card you have several options. Click the pencil to open the Flõ in the designer and edit it, Click the trashcan to delete the Flõ, or click the pages to make a copy of your Flõ. You can also click the ellipses to see more options.

You can quickly rename your Flõ from within the designer. You can also publish to Flõ to the Gallery to share it with other organizations.
