The Gallery is a great resource to find pre-built Flõs, get ideas, and learn more about the ways you can use Azuqua.

To use the Gallery, log in to Azuqua and click the "Gallery" link in the nav bar, or go to gallery.azuqua.com.

  1. Find a Flõ you're interested in using. You can browse trending, popular, and recently added Flõs or you can search for a Flõ based on a keyword (like a Channel, or a task).
  2. Once you've found the Flõ you want to use, click the "+" button to see more. Each Flõ published to the gallery has a title and a description telling you more about what it can do.
  3. Click the blue "Try this Flõ" button to open the Flõ in the designer.
  4. If you're not logged in, the Flõ will open in anonymous mode. Any changes you make will not be saved unless you click the Sign Up/Sign In button in the corner and log in to your account.
  5. Once you're logged in, the Flõ will be added to your account. Choose your own channel configurations, add more Channels or Functions, customize the Flõ however you want. Then, click the blue "Save Flõ" button to save your changes.
